My Story

In 2017 I earned my Bachelor's in horticulture and embarked on the six year long journey of running my own sunflower farm.

In 2023, while working on my farm's website, I realized I have just as much passion for CSS as I do for blossoms. After much soul searching, I decided to pull up my roots and swap fertilizer for Flex!

It's amazing how similar the work flows are between gardening and web development:

Meet with my client and discuss their vision.

Keep rows evenly spaced and clean.

Meticulously search for bugs and eliminate them.

Care for the :roots.
